Features
Everything field teams
have been asking for.
No more renaming files at 11 PM. No more "which site was that?" TimeSnap structures your photos before you even take them.
📁

Smart Folder Hierarchy

Project → Site → Category → Subcategory. Every photo knows exactly where it belongs. Metadata flows down automatically.

📋

Reusable Templates

Create a folder structure once, reuse it across projects. Camera settings, required shots, watermark config — all baked in.

⚡

Quick Capture

Open folder, tap, done. Auto-watermark with date, GPS, project name. Burst mode for rapid documentation. Zero setup friction.

🗺️

Location Intelligence

Every photo pinned on a map. Geofence-triggered folder suggestions — arrive on-site and TimeSnap knows which project you're at.

📤

PDF Reports & Export

One tap: photos + metadata + map → professional PDF report. ZIP export preserving your folder structure. Share per project or folder.

✅

Photo Checklists

"Capture 5 antenna angles" — with a progress bar. Never miss a required shot. Template-driven, project-enforced.

Organization

Folders that think ahead

Hierarchical folders with GPS, timestamps, and camera settings inherited per level. Your photos are organized before you take them.
